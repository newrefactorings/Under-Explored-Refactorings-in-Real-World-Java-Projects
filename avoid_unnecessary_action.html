Refactoring Definition:
Avoid Unnecessary Actions by Precondition Checking involves introducing a condition to check a prerequisite before executing an operation. This ensures that the action is only performed when necessary, avoiding redundant computations or updates.

Rationale:

  • Preservation of System State: Prevents inadvertent changes or updates that are unnecessary and could lead to unintended side effects.
  • Improved Clarity: Makes the intent of the code more explicit by specifying when an action should or should not occur.
