Refactoring Definition:
Remove Suppress Warning Annotation involves eliminating the @SuppressWarnings annotation when it is no longer necessary, such as when the code has been refactored to avoid the warning it was suppressing, or when the warning is deemed acceptable and does not require suppression.

Rationale:

  • Improves Code Clarity: Removing unnecessary annotations makes the codebase cleaner and easier to read.
  • Avoids Misuse of Suppression: Prevents overlooking potential issues by suppressing warnings globally or prematurely.
  • Enhances Maintainability: Reduces the burden of maintaining suppression annotations, especially when the underlying issue has already been addressed.
