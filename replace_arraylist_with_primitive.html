Refactoring Definition:
Replace Primitive List with Array involves replacing an ArrayList or similar collection-based implementation with a primitive array to manage a fixed-size collection of elements directly.

Rationale:

  • Readability and Clarity: For operations that require a fixed number of elements, arrays are a more straightforward and concise representation than dynamic lists.
  • Type-Safety and Explicitness: Arrays clearly express the fixed size and type of the collection, reducing potential errors related to dynamic resizing or unintended additions.
